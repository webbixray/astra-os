# Phase 0.6: Business Model & Monetization Strategy

## Pricing Model

### Tiered Subscription (Primary)

| Tier | Price | Target | Key Limits |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Individual, evaluation | 1 user, 1 campaign, 100 AI credits/mo, basic analytics |
| **Starter** | $49/mo | SMB, freelancers | 3 users, 10 campaigns, 1K AI credits/mo, basic workflows |
| **Professional** | $199/mo | Growth teams | 10 users, unlimited campaigns, 5K AI credits/mo, full workflows, analytics |
| **Business** | $799/mo | Mid-market | 25 users, advanced permissions, 20K AI credits/mo, custom integrations, priority support |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Large orgs | Unlimited users, SSO, audit, SLA, white-label, dedicated support, custom contract |

### AI Credits System

- Each AI action (generation, analysis, agent execution) consumes credits
- Credits are pooled at the organization level
- Unused credits roll over (up to 3x monthly limit)
- Overages at $0.01/credit (soft-cap with admin notification)
- Enterprise: custom credit pools with cost capping

### Add-On Revenue Streams

| Add-On | Price | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **Extra Seats** | $15/seat/mo (Professional+) | Additional team members beyond tier limit |
| **Marketplace** | 20% rev share | Third-party plugin commissions |
| **White Label** | $500/mo base + $2/seat | Agency reseller program |
| **API Access** | Usage-based | Rate-limited API tiers for custom integrations |
| **Onboarding** | $500-5,000 one-time | Guided migration and training |

## Go-To-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Product-Led Growth (Months 1-6)
- Free tier with core AI Command Center + campaign management
- Content marketing: "AI Marketing OS" thought leadership
- Community: Discord + GitHub + Product Hunt launch
- Target: SMB technical founders, indie marketers

### Phase 2: Community-Led Growth (Months 6-12)
- Templates marketplace (user-contributed workflows)
- Referral program
- Agency partnerships
- Target: Mid-market marketing ops teams

### Phase 3: Sales-Led Growth (Months 12-18)
- Direct sales team for enterprise
- SOC 2 + enterprise features unlock larger deals
- Channel partnerships with agencies
- Target: Enterprise marketing technologists
